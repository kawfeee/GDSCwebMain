WE ARE GDSC-CIT

Welcome to the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at Cambridge Institute of Technology (Main Campus)! Our community brings together students passionate about tech to learn, collaborate, and work on real-world projects. Through workshops, hackathons, and mentorship, we help bridge academic knowledge and industry skills. Join us to innovate, connect, and shape the future of technology!

Our mission is to empower students, foster creativity, and bridge the gap between technology and education.
